My Process

Design

Firstly, I will find out exactly what you want the website to do and what features you want in it. From this I will draw up wireframes, select an appropriate colour scheme and typography. Before I begin developing I will review the designs with you first. Just to make sure they fit your exact needs.

Development

This is the fun part. Now I will take the final design and with lots of coffee, I will transform it into a fully functioning product.

Optimisation

Although optimisation begins in the development phase, now through the use of online tools such as Lighthouse I can check the websites performance and tweak it as necessary.
